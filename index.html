To begin:

  • Blood gets filtrated at the glomerulus.
  • The filtrate (urine) then flows down the tubular system of the nephron.

The tubular system wall is composed of two sides, separated by a layer of cells (the walls of the tubule).

As we continue following the filtrate along each segment of the tubular system, you will learn that the properties of the cells in the tubular wall change.
